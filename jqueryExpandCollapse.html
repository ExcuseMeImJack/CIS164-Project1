My Story

From a young age, I was inspired by technology. What drove me to it was my favorite story.

When I was 13 years old, I attended a career fair at my middle school, and that's where it all began. As I explored the Project Lead The Way booths from the high school I would soon be attending, I was immediately drawn to a motherboard. I was captivated by the blend of green and gold, and I could feel the pricks of the capacitors beneath my fingertips. At that moment, I realized I wanted to learn everything I could about it all.

My Education

My past is a confusing one. Off-and-on college, bootcamps, and self learning. It's a lot to think about but every step helped me.

After high school, I chose to attend Cerritos College for a year and a half. However, I soon realized that I wasn't benefiting much from the curriculum, as I was already familiar with most of the material. It felt repetitive, and with the onset of the pandemic, I decided to take a two-year break. During that time, I enrolled in a bootcamp to enhance my software engineering skills. While I wasn't able to secure a position in the field afterward, I don't regret the experience. Now, I'm back at Cerritos, completing the necessary courses to earn my degree and advance my career.
